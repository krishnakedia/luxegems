Frequently Asked Questions

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM IST. You can reach us via phone, email, or WhatsApp during these hours.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with tracking details. You can also track your order by logging into your account or contacting our support team.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within India only. We're working on expanding our shipping options to serve our international customers soon.
